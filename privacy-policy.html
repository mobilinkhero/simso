1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you use SIMSO:

2How We Use Your Information & Legal Basis

2.1 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You give us explicit permission to process your entered phone numbers for the purpose of SIM lookup.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our app's security, prevent fraud, and ensure the service works correctly.
  • Performance of Contract: To provide the service you requested (instant SIM information lookup).

We use the collected information solely for:

  1. SIM Information Lookup: To query and provide information about the mobile operator associated with the phone number you enter.
  2. App Functionality: To enable core features of the application.
  3. Service Improvement: To improve our service quality and user experience.

3Data Processing & Storage

3.1 Ephemeral Processing Privacy-Friendly

Phone numbers you enter are processed ephemerally, which means:

  • Data is only stored in memory during the API request.
  • Data is retained only for the duration necessary to service your request.
  • Phone numbers are NOT stored permanently on our servers.
  • No database records are created for phone numbers.
  • Data is automatically deleted immediately after the response is sent.

5Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, VCDPA, LGPD)

You have wide-ranging rights regarding your data under various global privacy laws:

5.1 Right to Delete (Erasure)

You have the Right to Request Deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Since we process phone numbers ephemerally (they are not stored), there is typically no server-side data to delete. However, you can delete local data (search history) directly within the app or by clearing app storage.

5.2 Right to Rectification (Correction)

You have the Right to Correct inaccurate personal data. If you believe any app configuration or local user profile data is incorrect, you can update it within the app or contact us to correct it.

5.3 Right to Know about Sharing

You have the Right to Know whether your personal data is shared. We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

5.4 Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Targeted Ads

You have the Right to Opt-Out of the sale of your personal data or its use for targeted advertising. We do not sell your data nor use it for targeted advertising. Therefore, no opt-out action is required as we simply do not engage in these practices.

5.5 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the Right to Lodge a Complaint with a supervisory authority (Data Protection Authority) in your country or state if you believe our processing of your personal data infringes applicable law.
