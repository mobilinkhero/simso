At SIMSO, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following information when you use SIMSO:

  • Phone Numbers: The phone numbers you enter to check SIM information
  • Device Information: Basic device data such as device type, operating system version, and app version
  • IP Address: Your IP address for geo-location purposes (to provide region-specific content)
Important: We do NOT collect or store:
  • Your contacts
  • Your messages or call logs
  • Your location (GPS)
  • Your photos or media files
  • Any other personal data beyond what's listed above

2How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  1. SIM Information Lookup: To query and provide information about the mobile operator associated with the phone number you enter
  2. App Functionality: To enable core features of the application
  3. Service Improvement: To improve our service quality and user experience
  4. Regional Content: To display appropriate content based on your region
We do NOT use your data for:
  • Advertising or marketing purposes
  • Selling to third parties
  • Analytics beyond basic app functionality
  • Any purpose other than providing SIM information lookup service

3Data Processing & Storage

3.1 Ephemeral Processing Privacy-Friendly

Phone numbers you enter are processed ephemerally, which means:

  • Data is only stored in memory during the API request
  • Data is retained only for the duration necessary to service your request
  • Phone numbers are NOT stored permanently on our servers
  • No database records are created for phone numbers
  • Data is automatically deleted after the response is sent

3.2 Local Storage

The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • Search History: Recent searches are stored on your device for your convenience
  • App Configuration: Settings and preferences for app functionality
  • User Consent: Your consent status for data collection

You can delete this local data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app.

4Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure API: Our API endpoints use secure authentication and encryption
  • No Permanent Storage: Phone numbers are not stored in databases, reducing risk of data breaches
  • Regular Security Updates: We regularly update our systems to maintain security
